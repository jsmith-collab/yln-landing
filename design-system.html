Logo-led visual language

Refined, editorial, and rooted in the indigo monogram.

The mark suggests elegance, legacy, precision, and calm authority. The system translates that into a restrained indigo palette, warm parchment surfaces, classical serif display typography, and clean contemporary UI structure.

Brand Principles

These are the rules that keep the experience aligned with the mark instead of drifting into generic marketing design.

01

Editorial over app-like

Lead with generous headlines, restrained spacing, and composed rhythm. The brand should feel curated, not dashboard-heavy.

02

Indigo is the signature

The monogram is the strongest cue in the system. Indigo should carry the brand before any supporting accent does.

03

Gold is ceremonial

Gold should denote premium moments, highlights, and emphasis. Too much of it weakens the sophistication.

Recommended default application: use parchment backgrounds, indigo headers and CTAs, serif display headlines, mono eyebrow labels, and the full logo only in high-trust brand anchors such as navigation, hero identity, and system reference pages.
